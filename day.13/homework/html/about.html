About the Song

Released in 2019 on Tyler, The Creator's IGOR album, "EARFQUAKE" mixes genres like R&B, pop, and hip-hop. The song's emotional depth explores themes of love, vulnerability, and heartbreak. With its experimental sound and memorable chorus, it became one of the album's most beloved tracks.
