My Favorite Movies and Music

Coraline

Coraline is an animated dark fantasy film about a young girl who discovers an alternate world. While it seems better at first, she soon realizes that it is dangerous and must find a way back to her real world.

Music: Coraline Theme

Frankenweenie

Frankenweenie is a stop-motion animated film about a young boy, Victor, who brings his dog Sparky back to life, only to discover that the consequences of reanimating the dead are far from harmless.

Music: Frankenweenie Theme

Corpse Bride

Corpse Bride is a stop-motion animated fantasy film about a young man who accidentally proposes to a deceased bride while rehearsing his marriage proposal in the woods. He is then taken to the Land of the Dead.

Music: Corpse Bride Theme
