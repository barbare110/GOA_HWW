My Favourite Foods

Pizza

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a round, flat base of dough topped with sauce, cheese, and various toppings. It's baked until the crust is golden and crispy.

Pasta

Pasta is a staple of Italian cuisine made from durum wheat flour and water. It comes in various shapes and sizes and is typically served with sauces like marinara, Alfredo, or pesto.

Burger

A burger is a sandwich consisting of a cooked patty, usually made of ground beef, placed inside a bun with various toppings such as lettuce, cheese, and condiments.
